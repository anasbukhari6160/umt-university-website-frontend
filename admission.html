Admissions

Start your journey at UMT - we welcome bright and motivated students

Admission Criteria

Eligibility requirements for joining UMT programs

BS Programs (Undergraduate)
  • Minimum 50% marks in Intermediate (FSc / ICS / A-Levels)
  • Must have studied Mathematics or Computer Science
  • Valid NTS / UMT Entry Test score required
  • Original Matriculation and Intermediate certificates
  • 4 passport-size photographs
  • Copy of CNIC or B-Form
  • Character Certificate from last institution
MS Programs (Postgraduate)
  • Minimum 4-year BS degree in relevant field (CGPA 2.0+)
  • Must clear UMT Graduate Admission Test (GAT-General)
  • NTS-GAT score of 50 or above preferred
  • Original BS degree and transcripts
  • Two recommendation letters from professors
  • Statement of Purpose (SOP)
  • Copy of CNIC and recent photographs

How to Apply

Follow these simple steps to complete your application

1
Visit the Official Website

Go to www.umt.edu.pk and navigate to the Admissions section to check available programs and download the prospectus.

2
Fill Online Application Form

Register on the UMT admission portal and fill the online application form carefully. Select your desired program and campus.

3
Pay Application Fee

Pay the non-refundable application fee of PKR 1,500 via bank challan or online payment. Upload the receipt in the portal.

4
Appear in Entry Test

Receive your admit card and appear in the UMT Entry Test on the scheduled date. The test covers English, Mathematics, and IQ sections.

5
Interview (if applicable)

Shortlisted candidates may be called for an interview. Bring all original documents for verification during the interview.

6
Merit List & Enrollment

Check the merit list on the portal. If selected, complete your enrollment by paying the semester fee within the given deadline.

BS Artificial Intelligence

Bachelor of Science in Artificial Intelligence - 4 Year Program

← Back to Programs

Program Overview

The BS Artificial Intelligence program at UMT is designed to train students in building intelligent systems that can learn, reason, and make decisions. Students explore machine learning, deep learning, natural language processing, and robotics. This program prepares graduates for exciting careers in AI research, autonomous systems, and smart applications.

Duration

4 Years (8 Semesters)

Each semester is approximately 18 weeks long.

Total Credit Hours: 132+

Eligibility Criteria
  • Intermediate (FSc Pre-Engineering / ICS) with at least 50% marks
  • Mathematics is compulsory at the Intermediate level
  • Strong interest in mathematics and logical thinking preferred
  • Must pass UMT Entry Test (English, Math, IQ)
Key Subjects

Core AI Courses

  • Introduction to Artificial Intelligence
  • Machine Learning
  • Deep Learning
  • Natural Language Processing (NLP)
  • Computer Vision
  • Probability & Statistics for AI

Applied & Advanced

  • Robotics and Automation
  • Reinforcement Learning
  • Knowledge Representation
  • AI Ethics and Society
  • Neural Network Architectures
  • AI Capstone Project

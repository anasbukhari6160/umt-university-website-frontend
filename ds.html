BS Data Science

Bachelor of Science in Data Science - 4 Year Program

← Back to Programs

Program Overview

The BS Data Science program equips students with the skills to analyze large datasets, build predictive models, and derive actionable insights. Combining statistics, programming, and domain knowledge, graduates are prepared for careers as data analysts, data scientists, and business intelligence professionals in various industries.

Duration

4 Years (8 Semesters)

Each semester is approximately 18 weeks long.

Total Credit Hours: 130+

Eligibility Criteria
  • Intermediate (FSc Pre-Engineering / ICS) with at least 50% marks
  • Mathematics and Statistics background preferred
  • A-Levels with equivalent Mathematics qualification accepted
  • Must pass UMT Entry Test (English, Math, IQ)
Key Subjects

Foundation Courses

  • Python Programming for Data Science
  • Statistics and Probability
  • Linear Algebra
  • Database Systems
  • Data Structures
  • Data Visualization

Advanced Courses

  • Machine Learning
  • Big Data Analytics
  • Data Mining
  • Time Series Analysis
  • Business Intelligence
  • Final Year Research Project

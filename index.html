About UMT

Building excellence in education since 1990

The University of Management and Technology (UMT) is a leading institution in Pakistan committed to providing quality higher education. Founded in 1990, UMT has grown into a comprehensive university offering programs in Computer Science, Business, Engineering, and Social Sciences.

Our dedicated faculty, modern facilities, and industry-aligned curriculum prepare students for successful careers in a rapidly evolving world. UMT is recognized by the Higher Education Commission (HEC) of Pakistan.

BS Computer Science

Bachelor of Science in Computer Science - 4 Year Program

← Back to Programs

Program Overview

The BS Computer Science program at UMT provides students with a strong foundation in the theory and practice of computing. Students learn to design and develop software, understand computer systems, and solve complex computational problems. The program blends theoretical concepts with hands-on lab work and real-world projects to prepare graduates for careers in software development, research, and the IT industry.

Duration

4 Years (8 Semesters)

Each semester is approximately 18 weeks long.

Total Credit Hours: 130+

Eligibility Criteria
  • Intermediate (FSc Pre-Engineering / ICS) with at least 50% marks
  • Mathematics is a compulsory subject at Intermediate level
  • A-Levels students with equivalent qualification are also eligible
  • Must pass UMT Entry Test (English, Math, IQ)
Key Subjects

Core Courses

  • Introduction to Programming (C++)
  • Data Structures and Algorithms
  • Object-Oriented Programming (Java)
  • Database Management Systems
  • Operating Systems
  • Computer Networks

Advanced Courses

  • Software Engineering
  • Web Application Development
  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Computer Architecture
  • Theory of Computation
  • Final Year Project (FYP)

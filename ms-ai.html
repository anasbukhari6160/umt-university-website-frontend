MS Artificial Intelligence

Master of Science in Artificial Intelligence - 2 Year Postgraduate Program

← Back to Programs

Program Overview

The MS Artificial Intelligence program is a specialized postgraduate degree that focuses on the theory and application of AI technologies. Students dive deep into advanced machine learning, neural networks, computer vision, and AI ethics. The program emphasizes research and practical projects, preparing graduates for careers in AI research labs, tech startups, and industry R&D departments.

Duration

2 Years (4 Semesters)

Each semester is approximately 18 weeks long.

Total Credit Hours: 30 (Coursework + Thesis)

Eligibility Criteria
  • 4-year BS degree in CS, AI, Data Science, or a related field
  • Minimum CGPA of 2.0 (out of 4.0) in undergraduate degree
  • Valid NTS-GAT (General) score of 50 or above
  • Must clear UMT Graduate Admission Test
  • Knowledge of Python and basic ML concepts preferred
Key Subjects

Core AI Courses

  • Advanced Machine Learning
  • Deep Learning and Neural Networks
  • Computer Vision and Image Processing
  • Natural Language Processing (Advanced)
  • AI Research Methodology
  • Probabilistic Graphical Models

Electives & Research

  • Generative AI (Elective)
  • Federated Learning (Elective)
  • AI in Healthcare (Elective)
  • Explainable AI (Elective)
  • MS Thesis / Research Project
  • Research Paper & Thesis Defense
